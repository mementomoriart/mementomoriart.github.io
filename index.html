Lejla Pleho / Academy of Fine arts Sarajevo / Etching, printmaking

Inspiration for these works of art comes from the darkness that is in the very core of human existence; from the darkness, the anxiety, the feeling of isolation, and the solitude that exists in every conscious being because of the burden of existence and our struggle with ourselves. This state is not an exception, but is inherent in the very human nature that the modern man is compelled to exert from fear of condemination of those forbidden, taboo feelings and thoughts, the fear which ultimately leads to suicidal tendencies, the feeling of rejection and other deviations, which society categorizes as pathological.
